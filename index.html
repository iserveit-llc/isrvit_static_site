About us

Built by practitioners.
Powered by AI.

iServeIT was founded by practitioners who spent years inside the IT staffing industry — and grew frustrated that the tools, processes, and platforms hadn't kept pace with what technology now makes possible.

We are headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona, with delivery capabilities across the US. From day one, every engagement is backed by our proprietary AI platform, Plaxiq — built to bring intelligence, precision, and revenue discipline to an industry that has long relied on spreadsheets and instinct.

Our founding team brings deep relationships with Tier-1 technology organizations including Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Capgemini, HCL, and Cognizant, along with hands-on experience across every stage of the IT staffing lifecycle.
